Pre-meet

The picnic will take place in the park next to the Secret Garden Cafe in Bute Park. Bring along any food or drinks you fancy, a blanket to sit on and warm clothes!

Wedding

We'll be moving between venues on the day. So make sure to bring everything you need with you and wear shoes you can move in.
